Tourist Places

Unveiling the Hidden Gems of Riau

Istana Siak

Pekanbaru memiliki sebuah istana yang megah dan mewah bernama Istana Siak Sri Indrapura. Istana ini menjadi pusat pemerintahan kesultanan Siak pada era lampau.Tidak hanya menyajikan pesona bangunan yang megah dan menawan, Istana Siak menjadi saksi bisu perkembangan Islam di Riau. Istana Siak dibangun pada tahun 1889 oleh Sultan Syarif Hasyim Abdul Jalil Syaifudin. Deng an menampakkan arsitektur bergaya Eropa, Arab, dan Melayu.

Museum Sang Nila Utama

If you want to explore more of Riau’s history, you can also visit a museum in Pekanbaru. Sang Nila Utama Museum is located on Jalan Sudirman, right in front of the Riau Provincial DPRD office and next to the Cultural Park.

Alam Mayang

Alam Mayang is a tourist destination that’s perfect for a family getaway. Located just about 8 kilometers from downtown Pekanbaru, this attraction is easy to reach. Its main highlights are the natural beauty of the surroundings and the variety of recreational rides and activities avai lable for visitors.
