Local Food

Where Every Bite Tells a Story

Gulai Ikan Patin

Like most gulai dishes, you’ll find a thick orange-yellow broth with a savory and salty taste. The pieces of catfish are quite large, so you’ll definitely be satisfied when enjoying it. Fyi, you can easily find gulai ikan patin in almost any local restaurant.

Bolu Kemojo

At first, bolu kemojo was only served on special occasions, such as Eid or traditional celebrations. However, nowadays, this delicacy is often enjoyed as an everyday snack.

Lakse Kuah

Are you a coconut milk lover? Try lakse kuah when you visit Riau. This dish, made from sago noodles an d fresh sea fish, is a favorite among many.Not only that, you’ll also get to enjoy the delicious broth made fro m a mix of anise, coriander, and various other spices. Guaranteed to be mouthwatering!
